Our Company Story

Founded in 2005, V5 Medical LTD has grown from a small medical device trading company into a global leader in medical consumables supply. With over 150 dedicated employees and a network of premium manufacturing partners, we are committed to delivering high-quality medical products to healthcare providers worldwide.

Quality Certification

Certified by ISO 13485 quality management system, our products meet CE and FDA standards, ensuring the highest quality standards in the medical industry.

Global Supply Network

Products exported to over 40 countries and regions, serving hospitals, clinics, and distributors with professional medical consumables solutions tailored to local needs.

Safety & Reliability

Strict quality control system with multiple inspection stages, using EO or gamma ray sterilization to ensure product safety and reliability for patient care.

Complete Product Line

Covering over 20 categories and hundreds of specifications, providing complete solutions for hospitals, clinics and distributors with competitive pricing.

Our Journey: 20 Years of Excellence

2005 - Company Establishment

V5 Medical LTD founded in Kunshan, China with a vision to become a global medical supplier. Initial focus on surgical sutures and basic medical consumables.

2008 - First International Certification

Achieved ISO 13485 certification, marking our commitment to quality management systems. Began exporting to Southeast Asian markets.

2012 - European Market Entry

Obtained CE certification, enabling entry into European markets. Established our first overseas office in the UK to better serve European customers.

2016 - FDA Registration

Successfully registered with the US FDA, expanding our reach to North American markets. Invested in new manufacturing facilities to increase production capacity.

2020 - Global Pandemic Response

Played a crucial role in supplying PPE and medical equipment during the COVID-19 pandemic. Expanded product line to include protective equipment and testing supplies.

2024 - Present

Serving over 40 countries worldwide with a team of 150 professionals. Continued investment in R&D and quality improvement to maintain our competitive edge.

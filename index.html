Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services.

What certifications do your products have?

All our products are ISO 13485 certified, CE marked, and FDA registered. This ensures they meet international quality standards for medical devices and can be used in healthcare facilities worldwide.

What is your minimum order quantity?

Our minimum order quantity varies by product category. For most items, we accept orders starting from 1 carton. For custom surgical packs or large-volume orders, please contact us for specific MOQ requirements.

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery time is 7-14 days for stock items. For custom orders or large quantities, delivery time may be 2-4 weeks. We work with reliable shipping partners to ensure fast and secure delivery to 40+ countries.

Do you provide samples?

Yes, we provide samples for quality evaluation. Sample costs and shipping fees apply, which can be deducted from your first order. Contact our sales team to request samples of specific products.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept T/T (bank transfer), L/C (letter of credit), and other secure payment methods. For new customers, we typically require a 30% deposit with the balance due before shipment. We also offer secure local bank transfers in multiple currencies to minimize transaction costs.

PGA Absorbable Surgical Suture

聚乙醇酸可吸收缝合线

Our Polyglycolic Acid (PGA) sutures are synthetic, absorbable, sterile surgical sutures composed of Homopolymer of Glycolide (100%). The sutures are coated with polycaprolactone and calcium stearate to ensure smooth tissue passage and easy knot tying.

High Tensile Strength

Provides wound support during critical healing period (up to 30 days).

Predictable Absorption

Absorption by hydrolysis complete between 60-90 days.

Smooth Passage

Coated surface minimizes tissue trauma.

Secure Knotting

Excellent handling properties and knot security.

Technical Data Sheet

Composition Polyglycolic Acid (PGA)
Structure Braided (Multifilament)
Coating Polycaprolactone and Calcium Stearate
Color Violet or Undyed
Size Range (USP) 6/0 - 2 (Metric 0.7 - 5)
Tensile Strength Retention 75% at 2 weeks
50% at 3 weeks
Mass Absorption Complete within 60-90 days
Needle Types Taper Point, Cutting, Reverse Cutting, Round Body
Needle Curvature 1/2 Circle, 3/8 Circle, Straight
Sterilization Ethylene Oxide (EO Gas)

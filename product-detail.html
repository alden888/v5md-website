Product Description

V5 Medical's PGA Surgical Suture is a premium absorbable suture made from polyglycolic acid. This braided suture is violet in color, providing excellent visibility during surgical procedures.

The suture is designed for use in general soft tissue approximation and/or ligation. It is absorbed by hydrolysis within 60-90 days, eliminating the need for suture removal.

Our PGA sutures are manufactured using high-quality materials and strict quality control processes to ensure consistent performance and reliability in surgical applications.

Clinical Advantages:

  • Excellent handling characteristics and knot security
  • High tensile strength retention for optimal wound support
  • Minimal tissue reaction and inflammation
  • Uniform absorption profile for predictable healing
  • Available with various needle types and sizes

Usage Instructions

Indications:

For use in general soft tissue approximation and/or ligation where an absorbable suture is indicated.

  • General surgery
  • Obstetrics and gynecology
  • Oral and maxillofacial surgery
  • Dermatologic surgery
  • Urologic surgery

Contraindications:

Not recommended for use in cardiovascular surgery, ophthalmic surgery, or in tissues requiring prolonged wound support.

Precautions:

  • Do not resterilize
  • Use only as directed by a healthcare professional
  • Store in a cool, dry place
  • Inspect package for damage before use
  • Do not use if package is opened or damaged

Knotting Instructions:

For optimal knot security, use 4-5 throws for surgical knots. The braided structure provides excellent knot holding capacity.
